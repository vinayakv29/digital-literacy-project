**Social Media Do’s and Don’ts**

---

|Do|Don'ts|
|-|-|
|1.Use clear and respectful language in posts|1.Don’t use abusive or offensive language|
|2.Double-check facts before commenting or sharing|2.Don’t react or comment without knowing full context|
|3.Keep your profile information updated and appropriate|3.Don’t post misleading or false information about yourself|
|4. Take breaks from social media for mental well-being|4.Don’t spend excessive time scrolling mindlessly|
|5.Give credit when sharing others’ content|5.Don’t copy or repost content without permission|
