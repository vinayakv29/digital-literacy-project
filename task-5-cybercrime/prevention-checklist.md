##### **Stay Safe Online – Checklist** 

###### 

1. **Keep your devices updated**
Always update your phone, laptop, and apps regularly to fix security bugs.

2. **Download apps only from trusted sources**
Only download apps from official app stores like Google Play Store or Apple Store.

3. **Check app permissions carefully**
Do not give unknown apps access to your contacts, camera, or location.

4. **Think before you post online**
Do not share personal photos or location on social media.

5. **Log out from shared devices**
Always log out of your accounts on shared devices.

6. **Be cautious of fake job or internship offers**
Do not share personal details on fake job or internship offers.



###### **UPI / Financial Safety**



1. **Do not scan unknown QR codes**
Do not scan unknown QR codes as it may result in fraud.

2. **Check transaction details before confirming**
Always check transaction details before confirming.

3. **Never install unknown apps for “refunds” or “offers”**

&#x20;  Fraudsters may install unknown apps on your device to steal your data.
